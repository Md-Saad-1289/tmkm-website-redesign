About TMKM

Tamirul Millat Kamil Madrasah, established in 1979, is a pioneering Islamic educational institution providing Quranic, Hadith, and modern subject learning from primary to Kamil level.

Principal's Message

At Tamirul Millat Kamil Madrasah (TMKM), we are dedicated to nurturing the minds and hearts of our students. Our commitment goes beyond academic excellence: we focus on character building, moral and ethical education, and the development of practical life skills. Every student is supported in a safe, respectful, and motivating environment where curiosity is encouraged, critical thinking is cultivated, and creativity is celebrated. We believe in preparing well-rounded individuals who are not only knowledgeable but also compassionate, responsible, and ready to make a positive impact in society. TMKM aims to instill a lifelong love of learning while fostering discipline, integrity, and a strong sense of community.

— Dr. M. Khalilur Rahman Madani

Our Journey

1979-

Founded with community support, started small-scale teaching of Quran and modern subjects.

1995-

New academic building inaugurated, expanding classrooms, library, and exam facilities.

2008-

Achieved top board results, gained national recognition.

2020-

Introduced digital classrooms, smart learning tools, and global collaboration programs.

2024-

New campus planning and launch of enhanced student services.

Welcome to Tamirul Millat Kamil Madrasah

Since its founding in 1963, TMKM has been dedicated to providing a balanced education that unites Islamic teachings and modern academics.

We warmly welcome students, guardians, and well-wishers to a campus where knowledge, character, and discipline come together to shape a bright future.

TMKM is committed to building morally strong and academically skilled individuals.
